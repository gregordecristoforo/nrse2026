# About the Conference

Nordic-RSE is a grass-root professional association providing a networking platform for Research Software Engineers in the Nordic region. The 2026 conference features a unique two-day format:

**Day 1 (June 9):** Focused sessions for the RSE community - share experiences, discuss best practices, and connect with fellow RSEs.

**Day 2 (June 10):** Open to the broader community including students, researchers, and private companies - learn about research software engineering and its impact across sectors.

Whether you're an experienced RSE or just getting started with research software, this conference offers valuable insights and networking opportunities.

# About Nordic-RSE

Nordic Research Software Engineers (Nordic-RSE) is a grass-root professional association that provides a networking platform and community for Research Software Engineers in the Nordic region. We welcome anyone passionate about research software engineering, from developers and data scientists to system administrators and research supporters.

This conference is organized by RSEs, for RSEs, with a strong emphasis on peer support, knowledge sharing, and building connections across disciplines and institutions.

# What to Expect

Based on our previous conferences, Nordic-RSE 2026 will feature:

- **Keynote presentations** from leaders in research software engineering
- **Technical talks** on software development practices, tools, and techniques
- **Discussion sessions** on career development, community building, and open science
- **Tutorials** on practical skills and emerging technologies
- **Poster sessions** for networking and in-depth discussions
- **Plenty of time** for spontaneous conversations and community building

Our conferences are intentionally kept small (around 45-50 participants) to foster meaningful connections and conversations.
